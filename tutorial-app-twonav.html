App TwoNav

Install the map in the TwoNav app for Android/iOS using the account where you redeemed the product.

  1. If you do not have the app yet, install it on your phone.
    Android iOS
  2. Open the TwoNav App and sign in with your TwoNav account.
  3. Go to the 'Maps' list in the main menu.
  4. Tap the '...' button at the bottom-right, and select 'Install my purchased maps'.
  5. Choose the map and wait for the download to finish.
